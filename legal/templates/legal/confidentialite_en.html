Privacy Policy

How Vazimba collects, uses and protects your personal data.

admin_panel_settings 1. Data Controller

The data controller for personal data collected on the Vazimba platform (www.vazimba.io) is the Vazimba team, reachable at contact@vazimba.io.

share 3. Data Sharing

Vazimba does not sell your personal data to third parties.

Your data may be shared only in the following cases:

  • Technical providers: hosting (Railway), storage (AWS S3 / Cloudflare), transactional email (Brevo) — solely for the operation of the service.
  • Legal obligations: if required by a competent Malagasy or international authority, strictly within the bounds of the law.
  • Security: to prevent fraudulent or illegal activities on the platform.

visibility_off 4. Public and Private Content

On Vazimba, publications, comments and profiles are public by default and visible to all registered users. Your email address and password are never displayed or shared publicly.

Private messages (chat) are only visible to the participants of the conversation.

lock_outline 5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your data:

  • Secure HTTPS connection throughout the platform.
  • Passwords stored in hashed form (unreadable, even by our team).
  • Data access limited to authorised Vazimba team members.
  • Regular database backups.

manage_accounts 6. Your Rights

In accordance with personal data protection principles, you have the following rights:

  • Access: you may request a copy of your personal data.
  • Rectification: you may update your information from the Settings page.
  • Deletion: you may request the deletion of your account and data.
  • Objection: you may object to certain processing of your data.

To exercise these rights, contact us at contact@vazimba.io.

cookie 7. Cookies

Vazimba uses cookies that are essential to the operation of the service (authentication session, language and theme preferences). No advertising or third-party tracking cookies are used.

child_care 8. Minors

Vazimba is intended for people aged 16 and over. If we learn that a minor under 16 has created an account without parental consent, we reserve the right to delete that account.

update 9. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated to reflect legal developments or changes to the platform. Any significant changes will be notified to users via the platform.

mail_outline 10. Contact

For any questions regarding the protection of your data: contact@vazimba.io
