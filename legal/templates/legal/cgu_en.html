info_outline 1. About Vazimba

Vazimba is a social network designed for the Malagasy community, accessible at www.vazimba.io. Our mission is to give Madagascar an authentic voice — a space where Malagasy culture, language and values are respected and celebrated.

Vazimba actively contributes to the education and empowerment of the Malagasy population. The platform encourages knowledge sharing, constructive exchanges, and showcasing local talents and initiatives.

people_outline 2. Access and Registration

Use of Vazimba is open to anyone aged 16 years or older. By creating an account, you confirm that:

  • You meet the minimum age requirement.
  • You provide accurate information during registration.
  • You are responsible for the security and confidentiality of your password.
  • You will not create multiple accounts or impersonate another person.

block 3. Prohibited Content

In the interest of the community and the dignity of the Malagasy people, the following content is strictly forbidden on Vazimba.

🚫 Sexual or degrading content
Publishing photos, videos or text of a sexual, erotic or pornographic nature is prohibited. This includes content displaying intimate body parts (excessive cleavage, buttocks, etc.) intended to attract attention through provocation rather than through intelligence or talent.

Vazimba believes that a person's true value lies in their intelligence, creativity and accomplishments — not in staging their body. Such content will be removed and accounts will be suspended.
🚫 Violence and cruelty
Any publication showing acts of physical violence, torture or cruelty towards people or animals is strictly forbidden. These images traumatise community members and will not be tolerated.
🚫 Mob justice and calls for vigilante action
Publishing content aimed at publicly identifying, humiliating, judging or punishing a person (thieves, alleged offenders, etc.) is prohibited. Such posts encourage violence and violate the presumption of innocence. The Vazimba team will immediately remove any content of this type.
🚫 Political, judicial or punitive declarations
Publications intended to make partisan political statements, judicial rulings, or demands for sanctions or punishment against individuals or groups are not permitted. Vazimba is not a court or political platform.
🚫 Harassment, discrimination and hate speech
Any form of harassment, intimidation or discrimination based on origin, religion, gender, orientation or social status is prohibited.
🚫 Misinformation and fake news
Spreading rumours, unverified information presented as fact, and content designed to manipulate public opinion is prohibited.

star_outline 4. Encouraged Content

✅ What Vazimba encourages
Knowledge sharing, educational and professional advice · Showcasing Malagasy culture, art and traditions · Sharing job opportunities, training and entrepreneurship · Positive news, community initiatives and solidarity projects · Respectful and constructive exchanges.

We believe that an informed, educated youth — valued for their skills — is the key to Madagascar's development. Vazimba is designed to be that space.
